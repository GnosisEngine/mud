Dependency					Effect if broken
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Charcoal → everything metal			No smelting, no forging, no iron goods
Leather (bellows) → iron production		Can't run a bloomery without bellows — iron depends on hide/tanning
Fireclay (crucibles/tuyeres) → metal smelting	Specific clay deposit required for any advanced metalwork
